Appendix 2: Aquaculture Basic Universal List

The Aquaculture (Farmed) BUL tables are broken up into supply chain roles: Processor, Farm, Hatchery, and Feed. Because of space limitations, they are broken up by: KDE Definition, Data Format, Standards Data List and Standards Organization, and List Link, Authoritative Data Source, and Authoritative Data Document.
